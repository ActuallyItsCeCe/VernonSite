Meet Vernon Kirk:
Coach

Coach Vernon Kirk I is a Judo Black belt and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Purple belt. He has been training and competing since 2009. Coach Kirk is a lifelong athlete and a personal trainer and has been coaching since he was 20. He has won multiple Judo and Jiu Jitsu tournaments and has coached others to victory as well. Coach has lost 60 lbs because of the journey he started 6 years ago and is looking forward to sharing the arts that changed his life with you!

Vernon's Journey

"So in March of 2009 I lost my job. I was unemployed with a family of 4 to feed and in poor physical condition because I had been traveling so much over the past 3 years. I was 280 and had no direction in life. That June, I was looking to get my son into another sport. He did not like soccer and wanted to try something else. I went through the list, football, baseball, basketball and so on. He was not taking any interest until we got to karate. We went to the 1st of 2 local places and we were not impressed. We were getting ready to go to the 2nd when a good friend said, come to judo with us. We went and my son had a blast. We were getting ready to leave and my sensei looked and me said “Hey big guy, where are you going?” My response was “Home class is over.” He said “Adult class is starting” I reluctantly went out and was immediately hooked. I started going to class every chance I got and my weight started dropping and my confidence went up. By the end of the year Iw as down 20lbs and I had a new job. My journey has taken me to places mentally and physically I thought were passed me and I look better at 37 than I did at 27"
